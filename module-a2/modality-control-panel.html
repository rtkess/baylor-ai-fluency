AI Fluency for Faculty · Hankamer School of Business · Baylor University

MODE 01 Automation AI does, you review

What It Is

AI completes a well-defined task end-to-end in a single pass — generating a full first draft or output. Your role is to review, edit, and decide whether to use it.

In Practice at Hankamer

Prof. Marsh · Finance

Asks Claude to generate a first-draft amortization schedule template for a lending case problem set, then verifies the calculations before assigning it.

Prof. Duquette · Marketing

Has Claude draft a full set of discussion questions for a brand positioning case, then selects and lightly edits the ones she'll actually use.

What You Gain

Speed on repetitive, well-scoped work — freeing your time for the judgment calls only you can make.

Primary Watchout

A fluent-sounding first draft can still be wrong. It's tempting to skip the review step once the output looks polished — don't.

MODE 02 Augmentation You and AI co-create

What It Is

You and AI iterate together, each exchange building on the last. Neither of you produces the final result alone — it emerges through the back-and-forth.

In Practice at Hankamer

Prof. Iyer · Finance

Works through several rounds with Claude to refine a valuation problem set, adjusting assumptions each time based on what the last version revealed.

Prof. Okonkwo · Management

Co-develops a leadership simulation exercise with Claude across multiple exchanges, each round sharpening the scenario's realism.

What You Gain

A thinking partner that sharpens your ideas in real time — especially useful when you don't yet know exactly what you want.

Primary Watchout

You control when the iteration stops. Without a clear sense of "good enough," the back-and-forth can drift without ever finishing.

MODE 03 Agency AI acts on your behalf

What It Is

AI operates independently across multiple steps or over time, acting on your behalf without your review at each stage. You set it up, then check the outcome.

In Practice at Hankamer

Prof. Halvorsen · Marketing

Deploys an AI research agent that independently gathers and synthesizes competitor pricing data for an upcoming case study, reviewing only the final brief.

Prof. Petrova · Management

Sets up an AI-powered onboarding assistant that answers new MBA students' logistics questions automatically throughout orientation week.

What You Gain

Continuous coverage — tasks get handled even when you're not available to supervise every step.

Primary Watchout

Once deployed, AI is making decisions without you in the room. Institutional policy, data privacy, and clear boundaries on what it should never do all need to be set before you turn it loose.
